Campus Life

Front Of School

Has been provuiding education since 1830

Francis Howell Middle has been providing education since 1830. It has been named National School Of Character twice in the past decade. The school is a lively place with a variety of classes and good teachers. Every year the school welcomes 6th graders with Transition Day, which is what helps the newcomers cope with the new school. The school is an accepting and welcoming place.



School Achievements

Track Running
Winner Of FHMS Track Race

Winning the race is a good thing, as the Francis Howell Middle track is large-sized. The winners get recognition the following days, when the staff announce the winners' names on the school intercom.

What's New

  • New School Rules

    The 2022-23 school year at Francis Howell Middle will be reverted back to the original school rules, since the pandemic has gone and Covid-19 is low. The free lunches and masks requirements will be removed, and replaced with low-price lunches and no masks. These rules were to support families struggling during the pandmeic, and now that it has subsided, the rule is no longer in place. The school is also going to be stricter in terms of behavior. They will pay more attention to the actions of students and give them the proper discipline for their choices.
